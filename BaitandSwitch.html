Bait and Switch

  • This is one of the most common scams by cyber-criminals, these are usually found on popular, high traffic websites.


  • Attackers get advertising space from these websites and are usually in sidebars or pop-up panels. They get these spaces by giving the site administrator a legitimate-looking site.


  • After the advertisement goes live and is accessible,the link may send visitors to a page filled with malware. They can also be adveritising popular wigits or apps, this can run code on your device once you install it that can steal your inforamtion and ruin your device.


  • Your best bet with any online advertisements is to go to approved app stores, recognized brands, and vendors. If it looks fishy at all, do not click!
